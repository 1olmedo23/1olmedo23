Seattle, Washington, known for its iconic Space Needle and vibrant Pike Place Market, home to giants like Microsoft and Amazon. From hiking in national parks to exploring the waterfront, Seattle offers a dynamic mix of culture,nature, and innovation, making it a gem of the Pacific Northwest.

Olympia, Washington, the state's capital, exudes a unique charm with its combination of rich history and natural beauty. The city is nestled along the southern tip of Puget Sound and offers a tranquil waterfront setting. Olympia is a delightful and distinctive destination in the Pacific Northwest.

Known for its stunning riverfront, the Spokane River flows through the heart of the city, offering a picturesque backdrop for outdoor activities like biking and walking along the Centennial Trail. The historic downtown boasts beautifully preserved architecture, an array of fine dining options, and home to the iconic Pavilion and a serene, man-made waterfall.
